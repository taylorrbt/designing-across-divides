Get to know us

Whether we seek to address issues of governance, religion, race, the environment, economic development, education, or community health, the inability to communicate and cooperate in creative ways erodes social capital and weakens our ability to draw on diverse skill sets to address common challenges and respond to community opportunities.

Please join us in creating a professional development and showcase opportunity for community engaged scholars and designers and their community partners from all walks of life to learn more about integrating design, dialogue, and deliberation. These practices bridge divides between experts and the rest of us, as well as disciplinary silos between the arts and humanities and social sciences.
